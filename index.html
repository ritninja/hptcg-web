    Harry Potter TCG

    Welcome to the Wizarding World! Here is a quick guide to playing the Harry Potter Trading Card Game.

    ❤️ Your Deck is Your Health When you or your opponent take damage, you discard cards from the top of your deck. If a player's deck runs out of cards, that player loses immediately!
    ⚡ Actions & Turns At the start of your turn, you draw 1 card. You then have 2 Actions to spend:
    • Draw a card (costs 1 Action)
    • Play a card from your hand (1 Action, Adventures cost 2)
    • Use an ability of a card in play (costs 1 Action or is free, as specified)
    🪄 Card Types
    • Lessons: Provide magical power (Charms, Transfiguration, Potions, Care of Magical Creatures) needed to play other cards.
    • Creatures: Stay on the board and attack your opponent at the end of each of your turns.
    • Spells: Instant one-time effects that resolve and go to the discard pile.
    • Items & Adventures: Offer persistent bonuses or set challenges for the opponent.
